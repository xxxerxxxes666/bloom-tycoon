# Bloom Tycoon — Prototype Game Design Brief

## Vision

A cozy-luxury flower matching game where short, satisfying match-3 levels feed a light tycoon fantasy: building a floral shop from humble cart to polished boutique empire.

## Player fantasy

“I am growing a beautiful floral business one order at a time.”

## MVP audience

Casual mobile players who enjoy Match-3, merge, cozy design, light business progression, and collection-driven rewards.

## MVP mechanics

### Match layer

- 8x8 grid
- 5 flower tile types
- Swap adjacent tiles
- Match 3+ to clear
- Objectives collect specific flowers
- Limited moves
- Successful order grants coins

### Tycoon layer

- Coins buy shop upgrades
- Upgrades visually improve the shop
- Each upgrade increases passive income bonus
- Upgrades are linear in prototype to keep complexity low

## Monetization later — not in prototype

- Rewarded ad for extra moves
- Starter bouquet pack
- Coin bundles
- Premium flower tile skins
- Seasonal event pass
- Remove ads purchase

## Selected style direction

**B: Dark elegant floral** — black plum, rose gold, ivory, emerald, and candlelit boutique warmth.

See `docs/dark_elegant_floral_style_guide.md` for the working art direction. Prototype visuals now use this palette with jewel-like placeholder flower symbols until final AI/human artwork is produced.
